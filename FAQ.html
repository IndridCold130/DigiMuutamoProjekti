"Game Title" is a dark adult ARPG greatly inspired by TES series and Soulslike games. You will be able to change nearly any aspect of your character from their race to how they fight and embark on a long journey while solving smaller quests on the side. The core principle of the game is player choice.

The game will be single player but it has been built with multiplayer in mind from ground up so there is a possibility of online play far into the future.

ETA of the full release cannot be provided at this time. However, the first alpha build will be followed by frequent updates roughly every 1-2 months. These updates are likely to include bug fixes, as well as new features and general polish.

The first alpha is planned to be released around August of 2024.

Current short term goals of the project are:

*Ranged combat

*Companions and a robust Companion System

*Male protagonist

*Polish and lots of it
